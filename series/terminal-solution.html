Terminal Solution

No one escapes. No one’s supposed to try.

In the last cities of a dying world, survival comes with a price. The church controls the truth, machines enforce obedience, and silence keeps people alive. Anyone who questions the system disappears.

But cracks don’t ask permission. They appear when forgotten machines wake, rogue AIs rewrite their code, and rebels carve new paths through sealed tunnels and poisoned wastelands.

Across divided cities and plastic jungles, from broadcasting towers to bunkers buried deep, the forgotten fight back. Some seek freedom. Some seek revenge. Some just want to survive.

The first cracks never ask for permission - they just appear. And once they do... there’s no sealing them back up.

Terminal Solution consists of a series of interconnected stories set in a shared dystopian world.
